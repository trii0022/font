The process

The inspiration for this font came from various sources. At first I wanted to create something elegant looking that would suit some fashion brand, for example. So I was working with thin lines and tall letters.

Where did I get inspiration

I was inspired by Art Deco style - symmetrical, geometric, often simple. And lastly, while sketching the letters I found myself thinking that the geometrical shapes and straight lines remind me of the architecture that dominated Estonia in the 80s. This is why I decided to spell out Tallinn.
